What do these WORDS mean?

Front End

the stuff you see in FRONT of your eyes

Back End

the stuff you don't see because it's in the BACK

Full Stack

all of it, as in FULL house

Server

the thing that brings (yeah, SERVES) you thing you want

Router

the ROUTE (road, path) to the things (data, information) you want

Mongo

the big guy that carries all the heavy stuff

Mongoose

the little guy that wrangles the big buy and makes him do stuff
